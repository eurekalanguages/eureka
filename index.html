Eureka Languages Challenge

You'll hear a foreign word or phrase: As soon as you recognise it, click the phrase you think is being said. You only get one try and must answer within 10s.
There are 50 phrases in a 15m game. At the end we'll show you how much you understood of each of the languages that you heard.

Play somewhere quiet and use headphones. The harder you listen, the more you'll understand. Are you ready?
